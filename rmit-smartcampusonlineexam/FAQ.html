Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the online exam?
You can register by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and filling out the required information for either staff or student.
What are the system requirements?
You need a device with an internet connection and a modern browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.
Can I take the exam on my mobile device?
Yes, the system is responsive and works on mobile devices, although a desktop or laptop is recommended for best experience.
What if I forget my password?
Please contact your department admin or faculty to reset your credentials.
How do I contact support?
You can reach out to the technical support team via email or the contact section on our website.
